Fiserv — Aleph Hackathon

Clover is a Point of Sale platform that combines the best performing hardware available and Fiserv's payment processing standards, with an open App Market that any builder can use for shipping value added software to merchants.
🏆 Prize breakdown

Total Prize Pool: $1,000

    🥇 1st place — $750 (in USD)
    🥈 2nd place — $250 (in USD)

Additional perks for winners:

    Post-hackathon mentorship - Scale business oriented
    Pitch company and products to Fiserv top executives.
    Add App to the Clover App Market and build commercial agreement with Fiserv.

🧑‍💻 Use cases & track focus

    Build solutions for merchants based in the Clover/Fiserv infrastructure.
    Use Fiserv payment rails for FIAT payments.
    Ideally the solution uses AI and/or Blockchain at its core.
    Help merchants sale more, retain their clients, make their business more effective.
    Help merchants interact with each other and create more value.

🛠️ Tech requirements

Clover is Android based, Kotlin is recommended. A project template will be shared along with the Clover docs.
