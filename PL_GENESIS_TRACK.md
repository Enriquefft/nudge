🌎 Crecimiento Track

Crecimiento is a movement dedicated to positioning Argentina as a global hub for technological innovation, focusing on crypto and emerging technologies. It connects community, builders, founders, startups, international partners, and public stakeholders with the goal of accelerating technology adoption, expanding the entrepreneurial ecosystem, and promoting a stable regulatory framework that enables responsible innovation. Its work is structured through training and acceleration programs; community events and experiences; and strategic partnerships.

🚀 Accelerator Slot

One standout team from the PL_Genesis Hackathon will receive a guaranteed slot in the Crecimiento accelerator. The selected team must be willing to be in Argentina for the full duration of the hackathon and participate on the ground alongside the broader Crecimiento community. Through the program, founders gain access to capital, close mentorship, and a global network committed to building enduring companies rooted in real-world impact. Final acceptance and program details will be confirmed directly with Crecimiento.
