# Nudge — Hackathon Gameplan

## Time Allocation (~16 hours)

| Block | Hours | What |
|---|---|---|
| Clover setup + SDK integration | 3 | Emulator, project scaffold, inventory + order connectors |
| AI suggestion engine | 3 | LLM integration, prompt tuning, JSON parsing |
| Suggestion card UI | 2 | Card overlay, Add/Dismiss, auto-dismiss |
| Stats screen | 1 | Acceptance tracking, simple dashboard |
| **UX polish** | **3** | **Animations, typography, colors, native feel** |
| **Outreach** | **2** | **Visit merchants, record reactions** |
| **Demo video** | **2** | **Record, edit, voiceover** |

7 of 16 hours on polish, outreach, and video. That's intentional.

## Pitch Script (3 minutes)

**Opening hook (15 sec):**
"37% of restaurant staff never suggest an add-on. The ones that do are inconsistent. Merchants lose thousands in revenue every month from missed upsells."

**The product (30 sec):**
"Nudge is an AI assistant that lives on your Clover POS. When your staff adds an item to an order, it instantly suggests the right complementary product — with the exact words to say. One tap to add it. No training needed."

**Live demo (60 sec):**
Show it working. Item added → suggestion appears → staff adds it → ticket goes up. 2-3 times with different items. Fast, clean, no fumbling.

**The numbers (30 sec):**
"AI upsells on 78% of interactions vs 45% for humans. Average ticket increases 15-30%. For a merchant doing 100 transactions a day, that's $500+ in extra revenue — every day. The app pays for itself in the first hour."

**Traction (30 sec):**
"We talked to [N] merchants during this hackathon. Here's what they said:" → show quotes or clips. "Zero of them had any tool like this. All of them wanted it."

**Business model (15 sec):**
"$49/month. At 1% of Clover's 700,000 merchants, that's $2.9M ARR. Ready to ship on the Clover App Market today."

**Close (15 sec):**
"Merchants are leaving money on the table every transaction. Nudge fixes that. One tap at a time."

## Demo Video (mandatory submission)

Under 3 minutes. Problem → Solution → Impact.

1. (10 sec) Title card: "Nudge — AI Upsell Engine for Clover"
2. (15 sec) Problem statement with stats overlay + voiceover
3. (60 sec) Live app demo — 3 upsell scenarios, different item types
4. (20 sec) Stats screen: suggestions accepted, revenue added
5. (15 sec) Merchant quotes from outreach
6. (20 sec) Business model: pricing, TAM, ROI per merchant
7. (10 sec) Close: "Ready for the Clover App Market"

Record AFTER the app is polished. Budget 2 hours.

## Outreach Plan

**During the hackathon:**
1. Visit 2-3 local restaurants or shops
2. Ask: "How often does your staff suggest add-ons to customers?"
3. Listen — they'll say rarely. Record this (phone video, 15 sec each).
4. Show the concept: "What if your POS did this automatically?"
5. Record their reaction.
6. Ask: "Would you use this? How much would you pay?"

**Backup:** Call restaurants, do it over the phone, get voice quotes.

**Use in pitch:** "We validated with real merchants. 100% said they'd use this."
