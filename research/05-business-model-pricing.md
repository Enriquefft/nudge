# Business Model & Pricing Research

## Clover App Pricing Landscape

| Category | Typical Range |
|---|---|
| Simple add-on apps | $10-$29/month |
| Analytics / reporting | $29-$49/month |
| Loyalty / CRM | $29-$99/month |
| Enterprise / multi-location | $99-$310+/month |

**Sweet spot: $29-$49/month** — avoids sticker shock, "easy to approve" without manager sign-off.

Clover's VAS growth (27% YoY in Q1 2025) confirms merchants actively spend on software upgrades.

## Business Model Options

### A. Freemium
- **Pro:** Low barrier to install, builds merchant base fast, drives App Market discoverability
- **Con:** Free→paid conversion averages 2-5% in B2B SaaS
- **Fit:** Good for hackathon demo (show traction fast)

### B. Flat Monthly Subscription ← Recommended
- **Pro:** Predictable revenue, simple, easiest to implement in Clover billing
- **Con:** No alignment with merchant value
- **Sweet spot: $39-$59/month** (developer nets ~$27-$41 after Clover's 30% cut)

### C. Performance-Based (% of Incremental Revenue)
- **Pro:** "We only make money when you make money" — 18-23% higher retention than flat subs
- **Con:** Revenue volatile, hard to attribute, 37% longer sales cycles
- **Fit:** Most differentiated pitch for judges, but needs trustworthy attribution
- **Range:** 1-3% of incremental upsell revenue

### D. Tiered by Transaction Volume
- **Pro:** Scales with merchant success
- **Con:** Merchants hate feeling penalized for growth
- **Fit:** Good secondary pricing axis

### Recommended for Pitch: Hybrid
- Free 30-day trial (shows confidence)
- **$49/month flat** base (proven, market-aligned)
- Optional performance kicker: small % of verified incremental upsell revenue
- Frame as a VAS tool — directly speaks to Fiserv's strategic priorities

- AI tools command **20-30% price premium** over non-AI equivalents when ROI is demonstrable
- **87% of retailers** say AI positively impacts revenue
- **97%** plan to increase AI spending in 2026
- Key: merchants pay for tools that **make them money**, not just save time

## Go-to-Market

### Primary Channels (ranked)
1. **Fiserv/Clover sales team co-selling** — Fiserv has large sales force that recommends apps during POS setup. Hackathon win = direct entry to this channel
2. **Clover App Market organic discovery** — high installs + good ratings drive placement. "Featured" section is highly valuable
3. **ISO/reseller network** — bundle app with hardware sales
4. **Content marketing** — target "how to increase restaurant revenue" searches
5. **In-app referral/word of mouth** — powerful in local merchant communities, franchise groups

### The Hackathon-to-Distribution Path
Winning puts the product in front of Fiserv's partner/channel team. Fiserv wants to grow VAS penetration (currently 26%). A validated AI upsell tool fills a named strategic gap. **This is the clearest GTM advantage available.**

## LLM Cost Viability

Running AI features at scale is now extremely cheap:
- Daily business summary for 500 merchants (Haiku): **~$17/month**
- Real-time upsell suggestions (100 txns/day/merchant): **~$0.003/merchant/day** with cheapest models
- LLM prices dropped ~80% from 2025 to 2026

**AI features are commercially viable even at $29/month ARPU.**
