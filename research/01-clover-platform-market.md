# Clover Platform & Market Research

## Clover Scale

- **700,000+ merchants** globally (2025)
- **4 million POS devices** sold (mid-2025)
- **~$300B GPV** annualized, up 27% YoY
- **$3.3B revenue** in 2025 (+23% YoY), target **$4B+** by 2026
- Clover = ~25% of Fiserv's Merchant Solutions revenue but drives ~75% of its growth

## Market Share

- **~20% of U.S. small restaurant POS** card-processing market (~175K locations)
- Competitors: Toast ~17%, Square ~13%, Global Payments ~11%
- ~160,000 U.S. restaurant locations on Clover
- Overwhelmingly micro-businesses: 0-9 employees is the largest cohort

## Geographic Breakdown

- U.S.: ~94% of installations
- Canada: ~4.7%
- UK: ~0.47%
- **LATAM (live):** Argentina, Mexico, Brazil
- **LATAM payment processor:** SiTef (Mexico + Brazil)
- Argentina is the most mature LATAM market (multicurrency ARS/USD)
- Fiserv acquired Money Money Servicos Financeiros S.A. (Brazil fintech) in 2025

## Clover App Market

- **270-283 live app listings**
- **181-195 ISV partners** (80.8% U.S.)
- AI-powered apps = only ~3.33% of listings (nascent)
- Described as "the most extensive cloud-based app market for POS systems"
- Categories: Employee Management, Accounting, Inventory, Loyalty, Marketing, Scheduling + vertical-specific apps

## Developer Revenue Model

- **70/30 split** — developers keep 70%, Clover takes 30%
- Pricing models: subscription (most common), metered billing, tiered pricing
- Clover handles all billing, collection, and disbursement
- Developers paid in 3rd week of the following month
- No side-billing allowed — all fees must flow through Clover

## Fiserv Context

- **$21.19B total revenue** (2025), Fortune 500
- **#1 global fintech** on IDC FinTech Top 100 (3rd consecutive year)
- Serves ~10,000 financial institutions, 6 million business locations
- Reaches ~100% of U.S. households

## Fiserv Strategic Priorities (2025-2026)

1. **Agentic AI Commerce** — Partnerships with Mastercard (Agent Pay) and Visa for AI-initiated commerce
2. **Microsoft AI Collaboration** (Jan 2026) — embedding AI into Fiserv dev platforms
3. **ISV Ecosystem Growth** — pushing ISVs to treat payments as strategic growth engine
4. **International Expansion** — Japan (late 2026), Canada (TD Bank acquisition), Brazil, Australia
5. **New Products:** INDX (real-time settlement), Unknown Shopper (in-store analytics), FIUSD (stablecoin)
6. **"Project Elevate"** — CEO Mike Lyons simplifying the business, betting on AI

## Peru POS Market (field-validated 2026-03-21)

- **<5% of POS in Peru have inventory or catalog data** — they just track money
- Clover is not present in Peru
- Izipay and Culqi (largest POS providers in Peru) confirmed this gap
- Both expressed interest but said "we're not ready" — they want inventory management first
- Nudge requires inventory data to function, making current product unviable for Peru POS market
- This validates Clover's infrastructure (InventoryConnector, catalog) as a competitive moat

## Key Insight for Pitch

Fiserv's Value-Added Services (VAS) are used by ~26% of Clover merchants and growing 27% YoY. Positioning our app as a VAS tool directly addresses their stated growth strategy. The judges will want to see something they can put in front of their merchants.
