# Merchant Pain Points & Upselling Data

## Upselling Impact

- Upselling/cross-selling **increase revenue 10-30%** on average
- Combined strategies report up to **42% more revenue**
- **72% of sales professionals** confirm revenue growth from upselling — ~30% of their total revenue
- Upselling can increase **customer lifetime value by 20-40%** (HBR)
- **75% of businesses** report measurable revenue growth from upselling

## Average Ticket Increase

- Upselling alone can boost revenue **up to 43%**
- AI-driven recommendations raise order value **15-22%**
- QSR check sizes jump **26%**, fast-casual **13%** from upsell prompts
- Bundled offers increase AOV by **20-30%**
- Average restaurant check: **$28.50** in 2025

## The Upselling Gap

- Only **63%** of sales professionals actively upsell — **37% avoid it entirely** (WiserNotify — general sales professionals, not restaurant-specific)
- In restaurants, only **45% of human-handled orders** include an upsell attempt, meaning **55% of orders get no upsell** (Red Lobster/SoundHound 8-month study, via Hostie AI)
- **55%** believe upselling is their biggest growth opportunity but don't act
- **85% of customers won't respond to irrelevant cross-selling** (CEB)
- Key blockers: fear of being pushy, irrelevant offers, no training, no strategy
- **AI upsells on 78% of interactions** vs human staff at 45% (Red Lobster/SoundHound 8-month deployment, via Hostie AI)

## AI-Powered Recommendations

- Amazon's AI recommendations = **35% of total sales**
- **15-30% higher conversion rates** with AI vs static recommendations
- AOV increases **20-40%** with smart recommendation systems
- **AI-driven revenue-per-visit grew 84%** (Jan-Jul 2025, Adobe)
- **AI-based personalization improves repeat purchase frequency by 44%**

## Customer Retention

- Acquiring new customer costs **5-10x more** than retaining existing
- Acquisition costs **rose 60% over 5 years**
- **Increasing retention by 5%** can increase profits **25-95%** (Bain/HBR)
- **65% of revenue** comes from existing/repeat customers
- Returning customers spend **67% more** than new customers
- Success rate selling to existing customer: **60-70%** vs **5-20%** for new

## SMB Retention Gap

- **61% of small businesses**: >50% revenue is from repeat customers
- Only **34% of SMBs** have any loyalty program (even though they depend on repeat business)
- **45% of loyalty accounts are inactive** where programs exist
- True brand loyalty fell to **29% in 2025** (5-point drop)
- **82% of companies spend less on retention than acquisition**
- **44% prioritize acquisition** while only **18% prioritize retention**

## Loyalty Programs Work When Used

- **90% of loyalty program owners** report positive ROI (**4.8x average**)
- Top programs boost customer revenue **15-25% annually**
- Members who redeem rewards spend **3.1x more** annually
- **84% of consumers** say loyalty programs impact purchase decisions
- Small Business Loyalty Software market: **$1.35B (2025) → $3.02B by 2033** (CAGR 9.8%)

## Analytics Gap for Small Merchants

- Most SMBs use **Excel/Google Sheets or nothing** for analytics
- **65% of organizations** still making gut-based decisions (even higher for small merchants)
- Data analytics **accelerates decision-making 5x**
- Data-driven companies are **23x more likely to acquire** and **6x more likely to retain** customers
- BI implementations deliver **127% ROI within 3 years**

## Staff Training Problem

- POS can track **average order size per server** — reveals huge variance
- New employees lack product knowledge for natural recommendations
- Without AI prompts: upselling consistency depends entirely on individual personality
- **Only scalable solution: prompting staff at the moment of sale**

## Revenue Leakage

- **70% of in-store shoppers** make unplanned purchases due to upselling
- A merchant doing 100 txns/day at $25 avg who improves upsell rate by 10pts adding $6 per upsell = **$6,000 extra monthly revenue** from same foot traffic
- **83% of shoppers** more likely to buy with relevant suggestions

## Key Stats for Pitch

| Pain Point | Stat | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| Orders without upsell | 55% of orders get no attempt | AI removes human hesitation |
| Revenue from repeats | 61% of SMBs: >50% is repeat | Retention = protecting majority of revenue |
| Acquisition cost crisis | 5-10x more expensive | Retention ROI is overwhelming |
| Loyalty program gap | Only 34% of SMBs have one | Low-hanging fruit for AI-assisted loyalty |
| AI recommendations | 35% of Amazon revenue | Proven tech, not yet accessible to SMBs |
| Staff inconsistency | Wide variance per employee | Real-time POS prompts level the playing field |
